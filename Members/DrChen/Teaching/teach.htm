Dr. Chen has been active in both teaching and research. Right since his first term with Dalhousie University, he has been teaching various undergraduate and graduate courses in the areas of RF/Microwave communication electronics/systems, antennas and electromagnetics (5 courses per year on average), and has maintained good student evaluations.

The courses currently taught are:

  • ECED3300 Electromagnetic Fields
  • ECED4301 Electromagnetic Waves and Propagation
  • ECED4460 Communication Electronics
  • ECED6324 RF/Microwave System Design for Telecommunications
  • ECED6330 Computational Electromagnetics
  • ECED6999/7999: Direct Studies: Topics on RF/Microwave Circuit and System Design or Computational Electromagnetics

Other courses previously taught are:

  • Microwave Engineering
  • Antenna Theory and Design
