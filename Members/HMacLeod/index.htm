Overview

Heather’s current research is the design and implementation of a space- and polarization-diverse active antenna array for use in the ISM 2.4-GHz band.

In May 2005, she presented the paper “Experimental Studies of the 2.4-GHz ISM Wireless Indoor Channel” at the Communication Networks and Services Research Conference, in Halifax, Nova Scotia.

Prior to beginning graduate studies, Heather spent four years in industry employed as a Systems/Hardware Engineer.  During this time, she was involved in the design, implementation, and testing of various technology demonstrators for the Canadian military.
