Consulting

The laboratory is pleased to offer consulting services for members of industry and academia that have research questions related to the areas of expertise of our laboratory. Please feel free to contact us either by phone at (902) 494-6042, by fax at (902) 422-7535 or by e-mail at z.chen@dal.ca.

Please have a description of your problem that is as complete and as clear as possible. Also include in detail your full contact information. Your message will be acknowledged and your question will be discussed by members of the laboratory whose research interests are closest to your area of inquiry and give our best possible solution.

This service is offered by the laboratory to bridge the academia with industry. We promise to get back to you in a timely manner with feedback on your inquiry. Sometimes, however, a prompt response may not be possible due to travel and other professional commitments. If so, you'll be informed about the status and we will try our best to give you the feedback.

If you want to discuss your problems with us personally, or for other forms of consulting, feel free to talk to us to make an arrangement.
