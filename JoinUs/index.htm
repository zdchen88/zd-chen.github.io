Join Us

The Microwave and Wireless Research Laboratory invites you to join us if you are serious about academic excellence and research & development. Please send us the following information to help us assess your chance being accepted by our group (Email: zz.chen@ieee.org). However, please be noted that for the graduate study, this communication does not replace in any way or form the normal admissions process through the graduate admissions office; after our assessment of your records, you will still need to send in all the formal and official documents and application materials directly to the university by following the application procedure described at http://www.dalgrad.dal.ca/admissions/.

For Master's or Ph.D studies:

  1. Your resume
  2. Photocopy of your undergraduate or graduate transcript with GPA and rank in the class
  3. GRE and TOEFL scores if available
  4. Specific area of research interest
  5. Awards and Recognitions (if any)
  6. Your contact information (especially e-mail and phone).
  7. Electronic (.ps or .pdf) copy of representative publication(s) (if any)

For Post Doctoral Fellows and Visiting Positions:

  1. Your curriculum, including a list of main publications
  2. Areas of research and topic of PhD.
  3. Names and e-mail addresses of three references.
  4. Your contact information (especially e-mail and phone).
  5. Electronic copies (.ps or .pdf) of representative publication

Please note that due to a large volume of emails we receive, we are sometimes unable to reply to you. Therefore, if you do not hear from us 2 weeks after you contact us, it means that unfortunately we current do not have the appropriate positions for you. However, you are encoluraged to contact us 6 months later as our situation may change.
